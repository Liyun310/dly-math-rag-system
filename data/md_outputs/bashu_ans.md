# 14【答案】 $\frac{1}{4}$

【解析】由正弦定理,  $a\sin A + c\sin C = a\cos C + c\cos A$ 可化为 $\sin^2 A + \sin^2 C = \sin(A + C) = \sin B$ ,

曲 
$$S = tb^2 (t > 0)$$
 得:  $t = \frac{S}{b^2} = \frac{\frac{1}{2}ab\sin C}{b^2} = \frac{\sin A\sin C}{2\sin B} \le \frac{\sin^2 A + \sin^2 C}{4\sin B} = \frac{1}{4}$ , 当且仅当  $\sin A = \sin C$ ,

即  $A=C=45^{\circ}$ ,  $B=90^{\circ}$ 时等号成立,故答案为 $\frac{1}{4}$ .
